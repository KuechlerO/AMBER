Design of Base-Editing guide RNAs

The further analysis will identify biologically relevant amino acid substitutions and corresponding base-editing-compatible guide RNAs for your chosen protein.

All amino acid substitutions will be evalutated and ranked using AlphaMissense.

As editors, ABE and CBE are both supported. As PAM sequences, NGG, NG and NAG can be chosen.

Guide RNAs that target multiple positions can be filtered, deduplicated, or shown in full (see options below).

The Guide RNAs that span exon-intron boundaries are automatically excluded, as these would not function correctly in the processed mRNA.
