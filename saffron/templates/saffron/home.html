Signal-peptide base-editing guides

SAFFRON finds ABE/CBE guide RNAs that introduce missense changes inside SignalP 6.0–predicted signal peptides, then re-scores each mutant protein and ranks edits by change in SignalP scores (Δ).

Provide a UniProt / Ensembl ID for full guide design, or paste an amino-acid sequence for SignalP-only scoring (no guides without a CDS).
